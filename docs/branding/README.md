# Branding — NEST Platform

## Overview

NEST is a complete workforce automation platform. This document defines our brand identity.

---

## The Vision

**NEST** is not just another AI tool — it's a **complete workforce automation platform** that:

- Empowers employees to do more with AI
- Learns and evolves with your organization
- Replaces manual work with intelligent automation
- Works across coding, communication, research, and operations

---

## Positioning

### The Problem

Companies struggle with:
- Too many disconnected AI tools
- No visibility into AI activity
- Manual, repetitive tasks consuming employee time
- Knowledge lost when employees leave

### The Solution

NEST provides:
- **One platform** for every AI worker
- **Complete visibility** on phone/tablet
- **Automation** of repetitive tasks
- **Colony memory** that preserves knowledge

---

## Key Messages

### For Decision Makers

> "NEST: Your complete workforce automation platform. From coding to email to research — one platform, infinite possibilities."

### For IT Leaders

> "Self-hosted or enterprise cloud. Full control, zero vendor lock-in. Your data, your infrastructure, your AI workforce."

### For Team Leads

> "Every employee gets an AI workforce. Approve from your phone. See everything. Control everything."

### For Employees

> "Your AI assistant that actually does the work. Code, email, research, automate — all from one place."
